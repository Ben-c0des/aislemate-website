Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2025

Information We Collect

AisleMate is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

Data Usage

AisleMate accesses the following data exclusively on your device:

  • Reminders Access: To read and update your grocery lists with aisle information
  • Location Services: When enabled, used only for finding nearby grocery stores

Data Storage

All your data remains exclusively on your device:

  • Store information and aisle mappings are stored locally using iOS's secure storage
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No cloud storage or backups are performed by our app

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. Period.

Location Services

When you enable location services:

  • Your location is used only to search for nearby grocery stores
  • Location data is not stored or transmitted anywhere
  • You can disable location access at any time in iOS Settings

Children's Privacy

AisleMate is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from users under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will notify users through the App Store update notes.

Support

Getting Started

AisleMate enhances your iOS Reminders app with intelligent aisle organization. Here's how to get started:

  • Grant access to Reminders when prompted
  • Select your grocery list from your existing Reminders
  • Choose your store or search for a new one
  • Start assigning aisles as you shop - AisleMate learns and remembers!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to create a new grocery list?

A: No! AisleMate works with your existing Reminders lists. Just select which list contains your grocery items.

Q: Will this interfere with my current Reminders workflow?

A: Not at all. AisleMate enhances your lists by adding aisle information. You can continue using Reminders exactly as before.

Q: Does AisleMate work offline?

A: Yes! Once you've set up your stores and aisle mappings, everything works offline.

Q: Can I use AisleMate with multiple stores?

A: Absolutely! AisleMate remembers aisle locations for each store you visit.

Q: Why isn't location working?

A: Check that Location Services is enabled for AisleMate in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services.
